Lighting

Lighting is very important when it comes to photography and videography, it is one of the key components of setting up the mood of a scene. In DGA1019 and DGA1020 we learned the fundamentals of lighting and how we can use it to our advantage to create interesting scenes.

The Three Point Setup

The three point setup is one of the most commonly used setups when it comes to lighting up a subject or a composition. It is used a lot when it comes to portraiture, still lifes and product photography. This setup consists of 3 lights hence the name three point setup. The lights used in this type of setup are called the Fill light, Key light and Back light.

Highlights And Shadows

To create a nice balance of shadow and light one must pay attention to the:

  • The intensity of the light
  • The colour of the light
  • The positioning
